# Teams API

Team creation, management, and member administration.

## Endpoints

- `2/cs/teams` - Get all teams user is a member of
- `2/cs/team_profiles` - Get team profile(s) by team ID(s)
- `2/cs/create_team` - Create a new team
- `2/cs/update_team` - Update team information
- `2/cs/add_team_members` - Add members to a team
- `2/cs/remove_team_members` - Remove members from a team
- `2/cs/leave_team` - Leave a team
- `2/cs/team_settings` - Get or update team settings
